How it works

From SMS to insights in 10 seconds

No bank login. No app install. No data stored. Just paste and go.

01
📱
Paste your SMS
Copy a few debit SMS messages from your phone — the ones your bank sends when you spend money. Works with HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, and every other Indian bank.
02
🤖
AI reads and sorts
Our AI instantly identifies merchants, amounts, and categories. Amazon, Zomato, Swiggy, subscriptions — all sorted automatically with zero manual tagging.
03
💡
Get smart insights
See exactly where your money goes, get alerted on overspending, and check if your Amazon purchases were the best price — with live alternatives.
